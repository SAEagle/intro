Unreal Arena 3 - nostalgia project

The idea behind the project is to move Quake 3 Arena to UE 5. I've always wondered how it would be to play Quake with UE. In future planning add netcode to support local multiplayer. Here is the link to the repo where you can check the latest updates: https://github.com/SAEagle/unrealarena3

Knowledge:

  • C++
  • Q3A assets migration to Unreal 5
  • Blender
  • Niagara system

Side scroller Tech Demo

The tech demo for this project was built for the 3-4 months in my spare time: nights and weekends. The whole logic, animation states, level design, UI, and materials were done in blueprints. The initial prototype was used for fundraising and support later development. The project: Demon Skin. It was an interesting period of researchinh the 3d sidescrollers themes with interesting combat mechanic.

Knowledge:

  • Root Animations
  • Level desing
  • Blueprints

Hovertank Shooter prototype

Arcade shooter with strategy elements. At the beginning of the 20th century, ether was discovered, which replaced internal combustion engines. People have mastered gravity and after the world war, the world is in decline. You, as a representative of one of the governors of the current region, will have to restore order in the wastelands...

Knowledge:

  • Unreal physics
  • Trade system with trading UI
  • Open world map prototypes
